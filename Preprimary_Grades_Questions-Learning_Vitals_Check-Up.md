**Questions For Parents of Preschoolers / Preprimary** 

**Learning Skills Check-Up Preprimary Grades**

**Answer options for every question:** **Never** / Sometimes / **Often / Very Often**

---

## **SECTION 1: Thinking & Problem Solving Never, Sometimes, Often, Very Often**

1. Does your child give up quickly when something feels difficult? (toy/task/activity etc.)

2. When blocks keep falling, does your child try building differently, or give up after 1–2 falls?

---

## **SECTION 2: Attention & Self-Regulation**

3. Does your child find it hard to stay with one activity for **5 minutes** (colouring, puzzle, story, sorting)? *(FLAG)*

4. Does your child struggle to **wait for a turn** during play (snatches, rushes, interrupts)?

---

## **SECTION 3: Working Memory**

5. Does your child forget a simple **2-part instruction** (example: “Bring your red book & colour pencils and sit here”)? *(FLAG)*

6. The child struggles to tell you what happened in the correct order (example: what they did **first**, **next**, **last**)?

---

## **SECTION 4: Planning Skills (Executive Functions)**

7. How often does your child need a lot of help to **start** an activity (won’t begin without you sitting with them)?

8. Does your child move from one activity to another and leave things **unfinished** (half colouring, half puzzle)? *(FLAG)*

9. How often does your child misplace everyday items (water bottle, toy, shoes) and can’t find them without help?

---

## **SECTION 5: Posture & Body Management**

10. How often does your child find it hard to sit properly during table work (example: bends too close to the book, or keeps slipping/leaning and changing position again and again)?  
      
11. How often does your child stop quickly saying **“my hand is tired”** during colouring, drawing, or writing? *(FLAG)*

12. How often does your child seem clumsy (bumps into things, trips often, drops items easily)?

---

## **SECTION 6: Locomotor & Movement Fluency**

13. How often does your child avoid active play like running, jumping, or climbing (seems less confident, prefers to watch or stay aside)?

14. Does your child fall or lose balance during play? *(FLAG)*

---

## **SECTION 7: Coordination & Bilateral Integration**

15. How often does your child find it hard to do two-hand tasks (Example 1: hold paper with one hand and colour/cut with the other hand, Example 2: Pull pants/shorts up or down using both hands)? *(FLAG)*

16. How often does your child find it hard to build or fix toys that need two hands (example: one hand holds a block steady, the other hand joins another block)?

17. How often does your child struggle with action games that need timing (clapping games, “catch and throw”)?

---

## **SECTION 8: Object Control & Visual Tracking**

18. How often does your child miss when trying to catch a big soft ball or balloon that comes towards them slowly? (for example, when you gently roll or toss it to them)

19. How often does your child find it hard to follow your finger with their eyes when you move it slowly left to right in front of them? *(FLAG)*

20. How often does your child struggle with aiming tasks like pouring water into a glass, putting objects into a small box, or colouring within space?
